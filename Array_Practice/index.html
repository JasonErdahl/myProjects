About Me

My name is Jason Erdahl. I am 44 years old. My wife Kristy and I have 3 children. Elliot (16) is a sophmore at the Prior Lake High School. Abby (13) is in 8th grade at Hidden Oaks Middle School. Braden (11) is in 5th grade at Westwood Elementary. We live in Prior Lake MN where we moved in 2001.


I work at 3M under the umbrella of "Managed Services". My current job description is: IT Web Site Development & Support. 3M hired me in November of 2005 on a 6 month contract. 3M is a great company. They have an awesome atmosphere and a very strong innovative culture. My managers and coworkers are what make this place special. Everyone has their eye on contributing to the group. This attitude creates a culture of respect. 3M checks a ton of my boxes on what a company is supposed to be.


When I am not working I have a ton of interests. Spending time with my family and friends is top priority. If I have the time and money available I love going skydiving. It is an activity that forces me to live "In the Moment". It is fun, challenging, beautiful, and coming to terms with my mortality leads to a greater appreciation of life. Other interests include: running, mountain biking, golf, fishing, hiking and reading.
